ABOUT ME

WELCOME TO MY PAGE!

MY NAME IS MINA RAHIMIAN, BUT I GO BY VINA. I AM A PHD CANDIDATE IN ARCHITECTURE AND A PROSPECTIVE MASTERS STUDENT IN STATISTICS AT THE PENNSYLVANIA STATE UNIVERSITY. I AM AN AFFILIATED RESEARCHER AT THE STUCKEMAN CENTER FOR DESIGN COMPUTING AND MY RESEARCH FOCUS IS ON COMPUTATIONAL-SUSTAINABILITY. I'M OBSESSED WITH THREE WORDS: CITY, ENERGY, AND INFORMATION; AND TO SUMMARIZE IT ONE SENTENCE: I AM INTERESTED IN DATA-DRIVEN RESPONSES FOR SOLVING WIDER URBAN ENERGY ISSUES. I DEAL WITH URBAN SPATIAL AND ENERGY DATA FOR OPTIMIZING  COMMUNITY MICROGRIDS.

I LOVE RESEARCHING BUT TIMES WHEN I AM NOT DELVED INTO IT  I SPEND MY TIME JOGGING, COOKING, BAKING, AND READING BOOKS! ENTHUSIASTIC, ALWAYS UP FOR NEW IDEAS AND PROJECTS, AND I NEVER STOP!
